Questions, answered

Are these authentic designer pieces?

Yes — every piece ships from the studio or its authorised distributor with full manufacturer warranty. We sell overstock and end-of-line, never replicas or factory seconds.

How accurate is the 3D preview?

Models are built to true scale with calibrated materials. The temperature slider maps to real bulb kelvin values (2200K–6500K), so what you see is the light you'll live with.

What if I miss a drop?

Drops don't return, but studios do. Follow a studio and we'll bump you to the front of the queue next time they land.

Returns and shipping?

Free insured shipping over $150, 30-day no-questions returns, and every fixture is tested before it leaves the warehouse.
